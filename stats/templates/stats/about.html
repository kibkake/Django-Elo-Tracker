About

Purpose

Elo Tracker is an application designed to calculate and track players' elo rating across various office games. It can be a fun way to track skill progression for office games such as table tennis or pool and see where you rank among your other coworkers.


Registration

Creating an account is easy and only requires an email and password. Once logged in, you can register or join a company. If registering a company, you can invite other users by using the provided invite code. The main features of the app can only be accessed as a member of a company.


Creating/Playing a Game

Games can be created by pressing the "create new game" button on the home page. Once created, a user must join the game to be registered as a player of the game. After joining, players will be assigned an elo rating and be able to participate in matches.

After a game has been played, the results can be recorded in the detailed game view. (accessed by clicking on the game image or name) The Elo rating will automatically be calculated and displayed in the leaderboards page. Click on each players name to display their personal match history and elo rating changes.


Scheduling Games

Games can be scheduled ahead of time using the scheduler feature. An email notification will be sent to the scheduled players and the game will appear on the "Upcoming games" box in the home page.
